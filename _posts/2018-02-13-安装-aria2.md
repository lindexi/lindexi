系统是 Centos 6 32

首先安装 nettle ,注意版本

http://www6.atomicorp.com/channels/atomic/centos/6/i386/RPMS/nettle-2.7.1-3.el6.art.i686.rpm

安装 aria2


```csharp
    wget -c http://pkgs.repoforge.org/aria2/aria2-1.16.4-1.el6.rf.i686.rpm
    rpm -ivh aria2-1.16.4-1.el6.rf.i686.rpm
```
